Hello There!

Welcome to the Computer and technology Science Museum! In this website we have researched and prepared some of the most significant steps in the evolution of computer science and technology. Our modern lifestyle is directly connected to computers and it wouldn't be possible without these wondourus machines. As such, we believe that it is important to have some basic knowledge of their fascinating history.

Some of the things that we will briefly touch on this webpage:

  • About CPU's (Central Processing Unit). What are CPU's and why are they important, how are they made, what are some of the biggest companies that produce them?  
  • A brief history of computers. From ENIAC to the modern personal computer.
  • The development of software over the years. Specifically the Microsoft Disk Operating System,Unix along with linux and linux distros and finally the Mac Operating System. 
  • The different generations of Graphic Cards along with a short history of them.
  • Some general information about malicious software that people should look out for, the basics of protection, ransomware, viruses, computer "worms", spyware and so much more!
  • Finally, we have some information about storage media and devices. Either physical storage devices from floppy disks  to modern harddrives and SD cards, or  Cloud storage.
